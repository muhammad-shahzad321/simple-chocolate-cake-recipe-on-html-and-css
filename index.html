Grandma's Secret Chocolate Cake

This chocolate cake recipe has been in my family for generations and is perfect for any occasion.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup cocoa powder
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1/4 cup vegetable oil
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. In a bowl, mix sugar, cocoa powder, flour, baking powder, and salt.
  3. Add eggs, milk, oil, and vanilla extract. Mix until smooth.
  4. Pour the batter into a greased baking pan.
  5. Bake for 30-35 minutes or until a toothpick comes out clean.
